The Bitcoin blockchain was analyzed from 2009 up to November 2017 to estimate the uses of bitcoins.

The two main results researched are:
  • The monthly counts of transaction outputs belonging to different USD ranges
  • The monthly cumulate amounts of these outputs belonging to different USD ranges
The following transaction outputs are ignored for this analysis:
  • Miner rewards, known as coinbase transactions
  • Self-transfers of funds, likely to be single-input, single-ouput transactions
  • Change outputs, likely to be the largest output of a transaction
